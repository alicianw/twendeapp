ABOUT US

Founded in 2007, Twende helps you to join trips without having to worry about finding company! Choose a trip of your liking and have the benefit of having affordable and fun trips while networking and making new friends. Additionally, our coordinators are well trained to handle you in the most professional manner and thus you do not have to worry about any logistics.


PROBLEM STATEMENT

We noticed that individuals find it hard to travel to new destinations without company. Sometimes, the people they would want to tag along with them are committed and thus unavailable. We make it our responsibility to make group-traveling possible for such individuals!
